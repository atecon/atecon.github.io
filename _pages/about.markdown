Hi, I’m Artur!

I'm an economist who currently works as a senior data scientist at Europe's 2nd largest e-commerce retailer [otto.de](https://www.otto.de). My passion is for open-source projects and I try to contribute to one on a daily basis. I write most of my software in [Gretl](gretl.sourceforge.net/) and Python these days, but I am currently gaining experience with golang and Julia. An overview about my repos you find [here](https://github.com/atecon/).

Previously, I worked as a post-doctorate at the University of Hamburg doing research in applied macroeconometrics. I still do some research in this area. An overview about my research can be found [here](https://ideas.repec.org/f/pta322.html).

On this page you will (hopefully) find blog posts, additional documentation or insight, and talks about the projects I work on.
